Marina Barrage

Singapore's 15th reservoir, and the first in the heart of the city.

With a catchment area of 10,000 hectares, Marina catchment is the island’s
largest and most urbanised catchment. Together with two other reservoirs,
Marina Reservoir has increased Singapore’s water catchment from half to
two-thirds of the country’s land area.







Things To Do

Unaffected by tides, water level in Marina Reservoir is kept constant all year round.
This is ideal for all kinds of recreational activities such as boating, kayaking and dragonboating.











If water sports is not your thing, head over to the rooftop of the barrage, which boasts a wide green space.
With generally good weather all around, it's always a perfect time to fly kites with your family and friends.










If eating is your thing, spread your picnic mats on the well-kept grass. Spend some quality time with your loved ones,
with a spectacular view of the Singapore Flyer and the Central Business District skyline against the sky at sunset.
